Standard Operating Procedure for Image Annotation To provide clear guidelines for annotating images with cars and trees while excluding taxis from the car category. This SOP applies to all image annotators tasked with labeling images for machine learning and computer vision training datasets. Responsible for accurately identifying and annotating cars and trees in images according to the guidelines provided in this SOP. Responsible for reviewing annotated images to ensure they meet the dataset criteria and SOP standards. A record of annotated images, including the annotator's name, date of annotation, number of objects labeled, and any notes or issues encountered during the process. A log of quality assurance reviews, including feedback and any required corrections to annotated images. This SOP should be reviewed bi-annually or whenever there is a change in annotation guidelines or tools. The review will be conducted by the annotation team lead in collaboration with the quality assurance department.

Instructions and Guidelines

Step_Number Description Instructions Image_Example Example
1 Open Annotation Tool Launch the image annotation software and load the image to be annotated. open_annotation_tool.jpg
2 Identify Objects Carefully examine the image to identify all instances of cars and trees. Remember not to annotate taxis as cars. identify_objects.jpg
3 Annotate Cars Use the 'Car' label to annotate all vehicles that are not taxis. Draw a bounding box around each car, ensuring it fits the object as closely as possible without including background elements. annotate_cars.jpg
4 Annotate Trees Use the 'Tree' label to annotate all visible trees in the image. Draw a bounding box or use polygonal segmentation to outline the full extent of the tree, including branches and foliage. annotate_trees.jpg
5 Exclude Taxis Do not annotate taxis. Taxis can typically be identified by their distinct color (often yellow) and markings. If unsure, refer to local taxi identification guidelines. exclude_taxis.jpg
6 Review Annotations Review all annotations to ensure accuracy and completeness. Make sure all cars (excluding taxis) and trees are labeled correctly and that the bounding boxes or polygons are precise. review_annotations.jpg
7 Save and Submit Save the annotated image in the designated format and submit it through the appropriate channel for review or inclusion in the dataset. save_and_submit.jpg
