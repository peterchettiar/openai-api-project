Image Annotation Procedure for Cars and Trees To provide clear instructions for annotating cars and trees in images, while excluding taxis from the car annotations. This SOP applies to all image annotators tasked with labeling visual data for machine learning purposes. Responsible for accurately annotating cars and trees in images according to this SOP. Responsible for reviewing annotated images to ensure quality and adherence to SOP. Responsible for providing clear guidelines and resolving any discrepancies in the annotation process. 1.0 YYYY-MM-DD Initial creation of the SOP for image annotation of cars and trees, excluding taxis.

Instructions and Guidelines

Step_Number Description Instructions Image_Example Example
1 Open the image annotation tool Launch the designated software used for image annotation on your computer. open_annotation_tool.jpg
2 Load the image Import the image that needs to be annotated into the annotation tool. load_image.jpg
3 Identify cars and trees Carefully examine the image to locate all cars and trees. Remember not to include taxis in the car annotations. identify_cars_trees.jpg
4 Annotate cars Use the 'Car' label to draw bounding boxes around each car, ensuring the entire vehicle is within the box. Skip any vehicles that are taxis. annotate_cars.jpg
5 Annotate trees Use the 'Tree' label to draw bounding boxes around each tree, making sure to include the full canopy and trunk within the box. annotate_trees.jpg
6 Review annotations Double-check all annotations for accuracy and completeness. Ensure no taxis have been mistakenly labeled as cars. review_annotations.jpg
7 Save annotations Save the annotated image in the required format as specified by the project guidelines. save_annotations.jpg
8 Submit annotated image Upload the annotated image to the designated repository or send it to the supervisor for review. submit_annotated_image.jpg
