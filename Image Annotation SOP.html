Image Annotation for Cars and Trees To accurately annotate images with labels for cars and trees, excluding taxis. This SOP applies to the process of annotating images for machine learning and data analysis purposes. To identify and label cars and trees within the images according to the given instructions. To review the annotations for accuracy and adherence to the SOP. To ensure the annotated images are correctly stored and cataloged. [Ensure all cars (excluding taxis) are annotated with the 'Car' label., Ensure all trees are annotated with the 'Tree' label., Verify that no taxis are annotated as cars., Check that all bounding boxes are accurately drawn around the correct objects., Confirm that the annotated images are saved in the correct format and location.] Annotations should be reviewed for quality control upon completion of each image. 1.0 YYYY-MM-DD Head of Quality Assurance YYYY-MM-DD

Instructions and Guidelines

Step_Number Instruction Expected_Result Image Example
1 Open the image annotation tool and load the image to be annotated. Image is ready for annotation within the tool. image_annotation_tool_loaded.jpg
2 Identify all objects in the image that are cars, ensuring not to confuse them with taxis. All cars, excluding taxis, are recognized in the image. identify_cars_not_taxis.jpg
3 Use the 'Car' label to annotate each identified car by drawing a bounding box around it. Each car has a bounding box with the 'Car' label. car_annotation_bounding_box.jpg
4 Identify all trees in the image. All trees are recognized in the image. identify_trees.jpg
5 Use the 'Tree' label to annotate each identified tree by drawing a bounding box around it. Each tree has a bounding box with the 'Tree' label. tree_annotation_bounding_box.jpg
6 Review the annotations to ensure all cars and trees are labeled and that no taxis are mistakenly annotated as cars. All annotations are accurate with no taxis labeled as cars. annotation_review.jpg
7 Save the annotated image in the designated format and location as per data management protocols. Annotated image is saved correctly and is ready for further processing or analysis. save_annotated_image.jpg
8 Document the completion of the annotation process for the image, including any issues encountered. Annotation process is fully documented for quality assurance and tracking purposes. annotation_documentation.jpg
